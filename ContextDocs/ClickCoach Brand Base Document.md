# ClickCoach_Brand_Story_Document

**ClickCoach.io**

Brand & Story Document

Question 1: Tell Your Story. What life was like before you cracked the
code. The pain points of that life.

I sold my software company and then began helping other founders scale
theirs and prepare them for sale. That work made me a coach almost by
accident. As my results compounded, more people came to me asking for
help --- and within a couple of years, I was buried.

At my busiest, I was coaching five clients a day and spending more than
twenty minutes per session on admin: writing up notes, chasing homework,
building dashboards in spreadsheets, pulling stats together to prove the
work was actually moving the needle. Multiply that by twenty-five
sessions a week and I was losing nearly half a day, every day, to the
paperwork around coaching instead of the coaching itself.

I came to a fork in the road. Either hire an assistant to do all the
admin, or find software that could do it for me. I went the software
route and tried five different applications --- the ones every coach
knows by name. Some were too generic, designed for therapists or
trainers. Some were so feature-bloated they slowed me down more than my
spreadsheets did. None of them tracked the things I cared about:
assignments, accountability, measurable progress across multiple goals,
and the proof points I needed for testimonials and sales conversations.

After months of trying to bend other people's tools to my workflow, I
concluded the only way out was to build my own. So I hired a development
team and built the system I wished existed. That system is what
eventually became ClickCoach.io.

Question 2: What is the inciting event (how you stumbled onto the secret
that changed your life.)

Early in my coaching career, my results were hit-or-miss, and one day I
figured out why: I wasn't tracking the work I was giving my clients.
They'd agree to an action item on Tuesday and by the time we met the
following Tuesday neither of us could remember exactly what was
promised, what got done, or what didn't. So I started writing every
assignment down and reviewing it at the start of the next session. The
improvement was immediate.

Then a second problem surfaced. Even after a successful six- or
twelve-month engagement, clients would forget half of what we had built
together. They'd remember the breakthroughs, but not the path. I started
taking deeper notes --- every session, every realization, every
milestone --- so I could hand them back a record of their own journey.
The notes worked, but they piled up. Within a year I had hundreds of
pages of unstructured material spread across documents, files, and
notebooks, with no way to consolidate it into anything a client could
actually use.

The third realization was the biggest one: I had no statistics. No
before-and-after. No graph that said, "This client moved from a 4 to a 9
on revenue confidence, from a 3 to an 8 on team leadership, from a 5 to
a 9 on time management." Without that data, I was selling my coaching on
feeling alone, and feelings are easy for prospects to discount. So I
built spreadsheets and crude dashboards --- and that quickly became
unbearable to maintain by hand.

That was the inciting event. The secret wasn't a new coaching framework.
It was the discovery that the work of coaching gets exponentially more
valuable when every assignment, every note, and every result is
captured, structured, and made visible. That insight is the operating
principle ClickCoach.io is built on.

Question 3: What is life like today? Talk about the changes to your
quality of life.

Today, when I use ClickCoach, my day runs on rails. Admin time is down
from twenty-plus minutes per session to roughly five --- and most of
those five minutes are spent reviewing AI-generated session summaries
rather than writing them.

My clients now log into their own mobile-friendly portal that holds
every goal, every assignment, every note, every uploaded file, and every
progress chart we've created together. They come to sessions prepared.
Homework gets done because the system is asking them about it, not me. I
show up calm. I close sessions on time. And the work between sessions,
which used to be the weakest link in coaching, is now the strongest.

On the back end, my entire practice lives in one place. Scheduling,
billing, programs, courses, broadcasts, assessments, the coach directory
--- everything that used to be scattered across Calendly, Stripe,
Notion, Mailchimp, and a dozen tabs is now under one login with one
mental model. I can coach more clients without working more hours,
charge higher rates because the proof is built into the platform, and
walk into any sales conversation with measurable transformation in hand.

More than anything, I stopped carrying my business in my head. That
single shift --- the quiet that comes from knowing nothing is slipping
through the cracks --- is the change that mattered most.

Question 4: What motivated you to start your business?

Three things converged. First, the math: at five clients a day and
twenty-plus minutes of admin per session, I was burning roughly 100
minutes a day --- nearly two full workdays a week --- on busy work. That
was unsustainable both financially and mentally.

Second, the credibility gap. I knew my coaching produced results, but I
couldn't prove it the way a doctor proves outcomes with lab work or a
fitness trainer proves them with before-and-after photos. Without proof,
I was negotiating from weakness on every sales call.

Third, the testimonial problem. The best testimonials come from clients
who can clearly see what changed. Without a record, my clients couldn't
articulate their own transformation --- which meant I was leaving the
most powerful marketing asset in coaching on the table, every single
engagement.

I built ClickCoach to solve all three at once: cut the admin, create the
proof, and turn every engagement into a testimonial engine.

Question 5: What is the primary goal you have for your brand?

I want ClickCoach to become the single application that every coach uses
to run their coaching business --- the way Salesforce became the default
for sales, or QuickBooks for small-business accounting. The category
needs a category leader, and we're building toward that position.

The platform today does far more than the version I first built. With
Gemini AI embedded in the session workspace, a TipTap rich-text note
editor, a mobile-first PWA client portal, an operational reporting hub,
integrated file storage, broadcast messaging, in-platform assessments, a
unified calendar workspace, an in-app AI assistant, and a public coach
directory, we now support the full lifecycle of a coaching practice ---
from first inquiry to long-term client retention. And we do it at
roughly a tenth of what most coaches pay when they stitch together six
or seven separate tools.

My goal for the brand is simple: every working coach in the world knows
ClickCoach, and a meaningful percentage of them run their entire
practice on it.

Question 6: What drives your passion for your industry or niche?

I started a company from nothing, scaled it past one hundred employees
and ten million in revenue, served a quarter of a million customers, and
sold it for over ten million dollars. I know what that road feels like
--- the long stretches where nothing works, the inflection points where
everything does, and the operating discipline that turns a small
business into a real one.

Most founders and coaches I meet are closer to that arc than they
realize. They just need help seeing the shape of it, plus a handful of
frameworks they can actually execute. That belief is what pulled me into
coaching in the first place, and it's what eventually pulled me into
building software for coaches. After spending years inside the coaching
industry --- including running a 50-coach operation for Tony Robbins and
Chet Holmes --- I realized I had accumulated a library of frameworks,
scripts, scoring systems, and accountability models that most coaches
were trying to reinvent from scratch.

I've embedded that body of work directly into the structure of
ClickCoach so coaches don't have to build it themselves. Every coach who
runs their practice on ClickCoach inherits decades of operational wisdom
--- without having to live through the failures that produced it.

Beyond the resume, I have over a dozen detailed client testimonials ---

is designed to help every coach produce for themselves.

Question 11: Describe your target audience in detail: What are their
demographics, psychographics, and pain points?

Demographically, most ClickCoach customers are 35 to 60 years old, have
at least ten years of professional experience in their original field,
and have transitioned into coaching either after success in business,
leadership, or a specialty discipline. Some carry formal credentials ---
ICF, PCC, MCC, EMCC, or industry-specific certifications --- and many
are self-taught practitioners who built a coaching business off proven
personal results.

Psychographically, they are pattern-seekers and helpers. They take
detailed notes. They believe in homework, accountability, and structured
follow-through. They are skeptical of fluff and allergic to coaching
theater. They want their work to be measurable, not just inspirational.
They're willing to invest in tools that respect their craft --- but they
have been burned repeatedly by software that overpromised and
underdelivered.

Their pain points fall into four buckets:

- Operational drag --- too many tools, too many tabs, too much time
  spent on admin instead of coaching.

- Proof deficit --- they know they get results, but they can't show
  those results in a credible, visual, repeatable way.

- Pipeline anxiety --- without proof, sales conversations stall and
  pricing stays low.

- Cognitive load --- carrying every client's history, goals, and
  commitments in their head leaves them mentally drained before the
  workday even starts.

ClickCoach is designed to relieve all four --- and to do so without
forcing the coach to become a software expert in the process.

Question 12: What keeps your customers up at night? What frustrations,
fears, or problems are weighing on them?

The deepest, quietest fear is that they won't sign enough new clients
next month to cover their bills. Most coaches operate within one or two
clients of financial pressure, and they know it. Underneath that fear
sit a cluster of smaller anxieties:

- They can't articulate, on demand, why their coaching is worth what
  they charge.

- They've lowered their prices to close deals, and now they resent the
  clients they undercharged.

- They're working with clients they shouldn't have taken --- because
  they couldn't afford to say no.

- They suspect a competitor with worse coaching but better marketing is
  making more money than they are.

- They feel like an impostor sometimes, even when results say otherwise.

- They're scared their best clients will quietly drift away because
  nothing visible is anchoring the relationship between sessions.

What they don't see is that nearly every one of those fears traces back
to the same root cause: their value is invisible. ClickCoach makes it
visible.

Question 13: How does your company solve these challenges or pain

ClickCoach solves the coach's problem on three levels simultaneously.

First, time. AI-assisted note-taking, automated reminders, integrated
scheduling, and a unified workspace cut session-related admin from
twenty-plus minutes to roughly five. That recovered time goes directly

Second, proof. Every assignment, check-in, score, and milestone is

whiteboard, journal, or competing platform produces in the same form.

Third, sales leverage. Once proof exists, ClickCoach helps the coach
assemble that proof into testimonial stories, case-study assets, and
outbound positioning that can be shown to prospects on sales calls. The
same data that ran the client engagement now closes the next one. That
is the upward spiral the platform is engineered to create.

Question 15: How do you address these objections or concerns in your
marketing materials?

Each objection has a deliberate, evidence-based answer.

On cost: at \$497 per year, ClickCoach typically replaces \$1,500 to
\$3,000 of annual software spend across the tools coaches already pay
for. If \$497 is genuinely out of reach, that usually signals the coach
hasn't yet built a working practice --- in which case the priority isn't
software, it's clients, and we point them to free resources to get there
first.

On complexity: ClickCoach is designed for the coach, not the IT
department. Onboarding takes under an hour, the interface mirrors the
natural flow of a coaching session, and the in-app AI assistant can
answer setup questions in real time.

On time: every minute spent setting up ClickCoach is paid back within
the first two weeks by the time saved on session admin.

On size: even a one-coach practice benefits, because the platform isn't
priced or designed around volume --- it's priced and designed around the
daily realities of coaching.

On existing tools: most of the stack coaches use --- Calendly, Stripe,
Notion, ConvertKit, Typeform, Mailchimp, Google Drive, Dropbox --- is
replaced by ClickCoach features that are tightly integrated rather than
loosely connected. One login, one source of truth, one bill.

On implementation risk: we publish onboarding videos, offer office
hours, and the platform is built so that the very act of using it for
sessions produces the assets coaches need.

On data ownership: clients can export their data at any time. There is
no lock-in, contractually or technically.

Above all, the marketing reframes the conversation. ClickCoach isn't a
cost --- it's the infrastructure that lets a coach raise their rates,
prove their value, and stop carrying the practice in their head.

# ClickCoach-Feature-Overview

**ClickCoach**

Product Feature Overview

*For sales website & marketing • May 2026*

ClickCoach is an all-in-one coaching business platform built for
professional coaches and coaching companies. It combines client
management, live and scheduled sessions, accountability workflows,
client-facing portals, billing, courses, and reporting---so coaches
spend less time in spreadsheets and more time coaching.

## How to use this document

Features are grouped by business outcome (not by screen name). Each item
includes a short description suitable for website copy, sales decks, or
comparison pages. A final section notes what is standard across the
industry versus capabilities that differentiate ClickCoach.

# 1. Dashboard & daily workflow

The command center after login---customizable widgets, upcoming
sessions, and one-click actions.

- **Configurable dashboard ---** Coaches choose which insight cards
  appear and drag to reorder layout (saved per user). Optional widgets
  include active vs. inactive clients, broadcast engagement, course
  consumption, and team capacity.

- **Upcoming sessions ---** See the next week of sessions with
  pre-session brief, start session, and quick links to client profiles.

- **Quick actions ---** Start a live session, add clients, open calendar
  scheduling, and jump to reports without hunting through menus.

- **Ask AI How To ---** Built-in AI assistant trained on the full
  product---answers how-to questions for coaches inside the app.

# 2. Clients & prospects (CRM)

Manage active clients and pre-client leads in one place, with import,
groups, and conversion workflows.

- **Client roster ---** Searchable, filterable list of active and
  archived clients with profile photos, contact info, and session
  history.

- **Client profiles ---** Full record: sessions, notes, stats, groups,
  contracts, Zoom link, portal access, and custom settings.

- **Prospects pipeline ---** Dedicated prospects area with follow-up
  dates, outreach message history, source tracking, and
  convert-to-client flow.

- **CSV import ---** Bulk import clients or prospects; optionally assign
  groups and send onboarding packages on import.

- **Prospect source tracking ---** Tag leads by source (referral,
  webinar, ads, etc.) with reporting by source and custom reports.

- **Groups ---** Organize clients into cohorts for group sessions,
  courses, broadcasts, and group-level reporting.

- **Archive & lifecycle ---** Archive inactive clients while preserving
  history; mark missed meetings from the client list.

- **Multi-coach companies ---** Company accounts manage multiple
  coaches, permissions, shared branding, and optional coach directory.

# 3. Sessions & accountability (core strength)

Structured session workflow from scheduling through notes, homework,
Q&A, and automated client follow-up.

- **Live session workspace ---** Dedicated conduct screen with rich-text
  session notes and homework/assignments, timer, file attachments, and
  recording links.

- **Accountability question library ---** Reusable question bank
  organized in categories; assign questions per client and collect
  answers each session.

- **Generate questions from notes (AI) ---** AI reads session
  notes/homework and drafts new accountability questions for the active
  client.

- **AI notes clean-up ---** One-click rewrite of notes or homework for
  clarity and tone (preview replace or append).

- **Pre-session brief ---** Before starting, review last session
  summary, homework status, accountability answers, and quick stats in a
  side panel.

- **This week\'s focus (AI) ---** AI suggests priorities for the
  upcoming session based on prior notes, homework, and Q&A.

- **Session history & edit past sessions ---** Reopen completed sessions
  to edit notes/homework and resend homework email to the client.

- **Homework email templates ---** Branded email templates with merge
  tags; preview exact email before sending.

- **Send options ---** Control what goes to the client: portal access,
  notes, stats, reports, accountability Q&A, branding header.

- **Group sessions ---** Run a session for an entire group with shared
  notes and assignments.

- **Lesson plans in session ---** Open lesson-plan files from file
  storage directly inside the session workspace.

- **Autosave & drafts ---** Server autosave plus local browser draft so
  work is not lost mid-session.

# 4. Calendar & scheduling

Schedule coaching sessions and see them alongside external calendars.

- **Month calendar view ---** Visual calendar of scheduled sessions;
  click to schedule with client, date, and time.

- **Google Calendar sync ---** Connect Google Calendar---sessions can
  sync to the coach\'s calendar with details and attendees.

- **Microsoft / Outlook sync ---** Connect Microsoft 365/Outlook
  calendars for the same two-way visibility.

- **Timezone-aware scheduling ---** Scheduling respects coach and client
  timezones (platform-wide timezone support).

- **Dashboard → calendar flow ---** Empty-state scheduling routes
  coaches to the calendar with the schedule dialog open.

# 5. Client portal

Branded, secure login for clients to see progress, homework, stats,
courses, and invoices.

- **Client login ---** Each client gets their own portal
  (email/password) separate from the coach admin.

- **Portal themes & mobile layouts ---** Multiple visual themes and
  mobile navigation styles (tabs, drawer, compact bar, etc.).

- **Homework & session view ---** Clients see assignments,
  session-related content, and accountability questions to answer.

- **Stats & goals entry ---** Clients log custom metrics (up to 10 per
  coach configuration) with trend charts in the portal.

- **Courses in portal ---** Enrolled clients progress through modules
  and lessons inside the portal.

- **Invoices & pay online ---** View and pay invoices via Stripe (hosted
  invoice or checkout fallback).

- **Quick-access buttons ---** Up to six custom portal buttons (e.g.,
  Calendly, Zoom, resource links) configured by the coach.

- **Prospect-limited portal ---** Optional portal access for prospects
  before they become full clients.

- **PWA-friendly ---** Mobile-friendly portal with safe-area support for
  phones and installable web app behavior.

# 6. Assessments & evaluations

Beyond session Q&A---a full assessment system with libraries, templates,
and client responses.

- **Accountability tab ---** Session question library (see
  Sessions)---primary place to manage categories and portal visibility.

- **Performance & evaluation library ---** Ready-made evaluation
  templates coaches can preview, customize, and add to their library.

- **My assessments ---** Custom assessments, copies from templates, and
  a standard starter set for new accounts.

- **Assign & collect responses ---** Assign assessments to clients; view
  responses in the coach admin.

- **Custom terminology ---** Rename labels (e.g., \'Accountability
  Questions,\' \'Homework\') across coach app and portal.

# 7. Stats, goals & progress tracking

Custom metrics and charts---stronger depth than lightweight
scheduling-only tools.

- **Up to 10 custom metrics ---** Coaches define stat names, colors, and
  which metrics are enabled globally or per client.

- **Client data entry ---** Clients enter values in the portal; coaches
  see trends in session workspace and reports.

- **Charts in session ---** Stacked mini charts with min/max/range and
  scrollable entry history beside live sessions.

- **2D/3D chart styles ---** Configurable chart presentation for stats
  visualization.

- **Monthly stats report ---** Cross-client comparison of stat
  performance over time.

- **Stats & goals page ---** Dedicated client and coach views for deeper
  analysis.

# 8. Learning (courses)

Build and deliver structured programs inside the platform---not just
session notes.

- **Courses, modules & lessons ---** Hierarchical course builder with
  rich text and embedded video.

- **Enroll clients or groups ---** Enroll individuals or entire groups;
  optional auto-enroll when new members join a group.

- **Progress tracking ---** See learning consumption on dashboard
  insights and course management screens.

- **Portal learning area ---** Clients access enrolled courses from
  their portal navigation.

# 9. Billing, payments & subscriptions

Coach-to-client money collection integrated with Stripe---not a separate
invoicing tool.

- **Stripe integration ---** Invoices, subscriptions, and client
  payments processed through Stripe.

- **Send invoices ---** Create line-item invoices, set due dates, email
  clients with branded templates.

- **Client pay in portal ---** Clients pay open invoices from the portal
  (Pay Now).

- **Subscription products ---** Recurring billing plans for ongoing
  coaching packages.

- **Payment history ---** Track payments and receipts per client.

- **ClickCoach platform billing ---** Coaches subscribe to ClickCoach
  itself (plans, usage, payment method).

- **E-signature / contracts ---** Send coaching agreements for
  electronic signature; sample agreements included for new accounts.

- **Invoice & homework email templates ---** Separate template editors
  for invoice emails and homework emails with merge tags.

# 10. Communication & outreach

Stay in touch between sessions without a separate email marketing tool.

- **Broadcast email ---** One branded email to active clients, archived
  clients, or prospects---optionally filtered by group.

- **Attachments ---** Attach files from coach file storage or Google
  Drive picker.

- **Email preview ---** Preview broadcast layout before sending.

- **Prospect outreach log ---** Track individual messages sent to
  prospects from the prospects dashboard.

- **Postmark delivery ---** Reliable transactional and broadcast email
  delivery.

- **Branded emails ---** Company logo, header colors, and alignment on
  outbound messages.

# 11. File storage & resources

Central document library for coaches and clients.

- **Coach file storage ---** Upload PDFs, Office docs, images, and more
  (quota-based).

- **Lesson plan flag ---** Mark files as lesson plans for quick access
  during sessions.

- **Client-associated files ---** Share files with specific clients via
  portal or sessions.

- **Google Drive picker ---** Attach Drive files to broadcasts (and
  prospect messages) without re-uploading.

- **Sample contracts ---** Pre-loaded sample coaching agreements for new
  company/coach accounts.

# 12. Reports & analytics

Operational and business visibility across clients, coaches, and
prospects.

- **Monthly stats report ---** All clients\' metrics in one view with
  charts.

- **Coach & client productivity ---** Activity levels for coaches and
  clients.

- **Attendance report ---** Session attendance patterns.

- **Missing sessions report ---** Find missed sessions by date range and
  coach.

- **Onboarding report ---** Track onboarding package completion.

- **Feedback reports ---** Client feedback and satisfaction data.

- **Prospect source report ---** Leads grouped and filtered by marketing
  source.

- **Prospects pipeline report ---** Pipeline-oriented prospect
  analytics.

- **Custom report builder ---** Choose fields and export session or
  prospect data (CSV-oriented workflow).

- **Company calendar view ---** Multi-coach companies see aggregated
  calendar events.

# 13. AI-powered features

Practical AI embedded in coaching workflows (Google Gemini).

- **Ask AI How To ---** In-app guide for feature questions.

- **AI notes & homework clean-up ---** Improve clarity and
  professionalism of session content.

- **Generate accountability questions ---** Turn session content into
  accountability prompts automatically.

- **Session focus suggestion ---** AI-generated \'this week\'s focus\'
  for the next session.

- **Session focus extract ---** Summarize priorities from session
  materials on detail pages.

# 14. Branding, teams & white-label

Built for solo coaches and coaching companies that need brand control.

- **Company branding ---** Logo, header colors, logo alignment on emails
  and portal.

- **White-label / partner URLs ---** Partner-specific URLs and branding
  for multi-tenant deployments.

- **Multi-coach teams ---** Add coaches under one company; per-coach
  permissions (billing, reports, courses, etc.).

- **Delegated admin ---** One trusted delegate login with granular
  section access.

- **Public coach directory ---** Optional public directory of coaches
  with bios and testimonials.

- **Coach profile ---** Rich coach profiles with tags, testimonials, and
  directory inclusion toggle.

- **Custom SMTP ---** Optional custom outbound email configuration for
  companies.

# 15. Integrations & automation

Connect calendars, CRMs, and automation tools.

- **Google Calendar ---** Read/write session events on Google Calendar.

- **Microsoft Outlook / 365 ---** Calendar sync for Outlook users.

- **Webhooks (outbound) ---** Fire webhooks on session complete, client
  created, invoice paid, assignment added, invoice sent.

- **API key (inbound) ---** Generate API keys for custom integrations.

- **GoHighLevel & Groove.cm ---** Built-in endpoint configuration for

- **Stripe ---** Payments, subscriptions, and Connect-style coach

- **Zoom links ---** Store per-client Zoom links on profiles for quick

# 16. Onboarding & client experience extras

Smooth first impression for new clients.

- **Onboarding packages ---** Multi-step onboarding with rich text and
  file attachments; assign on import or manually.

- **Conversion from prospect ---** Convert prospect to client with
  optional welcome email (customizable).

- **Comments on homework ---** Token-based comment threads so clients
  can ask questions on assignments.

- **Two-way comments setting ---** Company-level option for comment
  workflows.

- **Secure login & password reset ---** Standard auth with email reset;
  role-based access (coach, company, admin, client).

- **Interactive help center ---** In-app Help with FAQ and tabbed guides
