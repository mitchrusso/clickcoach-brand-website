Privacy & data ownership

Your client data is yours. We don't sell it. We don't share it. You can export everything in one click any time, and your clients can do the same with their own portal data. The platform is designed to be left as easily as it is joined.

Terms of service

Annual subscription, billed up front, cancel any time before renewal. No long-term contracts, no setup fees, no surprise upcharges. Full terms available on request — we believe a fair business doesn't hide them.

How fast do you respond?

Sales inquiries: within one business day, usually same-day. Support tickets: within four business hours during US business hours, with the in-app AI assistant available 24/7 for setup questions.

Will Mitch personally talk to me?

For partner, white-label, and certification-network inquiries — yes. For solo-coach sales calls, our team handles it (we built a product so you wouldn't have to wait on one person). If you want hands-on 1:1 work with Mitch directly, visit MitchRusso.com.
