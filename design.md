## Brand & Style
Professional/Modern SaaS aesthetic — "High-Clarity Minimalism." Generous whitespace, crisp geometric shapes, utility-first trust palette. Clarity, Trust, Vibrancy. EmailOctopus-inspired layout discipline.

## Colors
- Primary #2463EB — buttons, active states, brand markers.
- Secondary #0F172A — nav, headings, footers.
- Tertiary #D9F99D (lime) — high-attention callouts, "New" badges, Sign-Up button.
- Backgrounds #F8FAFC with #F1F5F9 for container differentiation.

## Typography
- Montserrat for headlines (display, headline-md/sm), tight letter-spacing on large sizes.
- Plus Jakarta Sans for body, UI labels, forms. Semi-bold labels.
- display-lg 48/36px mobile; headline-md 32px; body-md 16px line-height 1.6.

## Layout
12-col grid, 24px gutter, 1200px max. 80px section gaps desktop / 32px mobile. 8px spacing base. Content stacks on mobile.

## Elevation
Tonal layering + 1px outlines (#E2E8F0). No heavy shadows. Ambient hover shadow: 0 4px 20px -2px rgba(15,23,42,.08). Cards flat at rest, lift 2px on hover.

## Shapes
Rounded by default. 8px base radius (buttons/inputs/cards), 16px for hero containers, fully rounded for chips/badges/avatars.

## Components
- Primary button: #2463EB bg, white text, 8px radius, bold Montserrat.
- Secondary button: transparent w/ #2463EB border + text.
- Accent CTA: #D9F99D bg, #0C0C0D text — Sign Up.
- Inputs: white, 1px #E2E8F0 border, focus = primary blue + 2px 15% glow.
- Cards: white + subtle border, ambient shadow on hover.
- Chips: pill-shaped, tinted with 10% status color.

## Imagery (designer-led)
Real photography — coaches working with clients, mobile-first product screens, candid 1:1 sessions, founder portrait. Avoid stock "diverse business team pointing at laptop." Layer in product UI screenshots as flat cards floated into hero compositions. Lime accent should appear sparingly in geometric/illustrative motifs (badges, arrows, underlines).
