14 capability buckets · one login

Features grouped the way coaches actually work.

Not by screen name — by business outcome. Every block below replaces a tool you probably already pay for.

🗓️

Sessions & accountability

Live session workspace with rich-text notes, homework, timer, file attachments, recording links. Reusable accountability question library, organized by category. AI generates new questions from your notes — and cleans up your prose before homework goes out.

  • Pre-session brief: last summary, homework status, stats, Q&A in one panel
  • "This week's focus" — AI surfaces priorities for next session
  • Session history with edit + resend homework email
  • Branded homework email templates with merge tags
👥

Clients, prospects & CRM

A searchable client roster with full profiles, plus a real prospects pipeline (follow-ups, outreach history, source tracking, convert-to-client flow). CSV import. Groups for cohorts. Archive without losing history.

  • Built-in prospects pipeline — kill your separate CRM
  • Source-attribution reporting on every prospect
  • Groups for cohort programs, broadcasts, group-level reports
  • Multi-coach company accounts with permissions
📱

Client portal (PWA)

Each client gets a branded mobile-first portal with homework, stats entry, courses, invoices, and quick-access buttons. Multiple themes. Safe-area support. Installable like a native app.

  • Clients log goals + 10 custom metrics from their phone
  • Pay invoices via Stripe in-portal
  • Up to six configurable quick-access buttons per coach
  • Prospect-limited portal access before they convert
📈

Stats, goals & progress

Up to 10 custom metrics, named and colored by you. Clients enter values. You see stacked mini-charts beside your live session notes. Monthly stats report compares all clients over time.

  • 2D / 3D chart styles, configurable per metric
  • Min/max/range visible during live sessions
  • Dedicated stats & goals page for deeper analysis
  • The chart is your testimonial — clients can see what changed
🎓

Learning (courses)

Hierarchical course builder — courses → modules → lessons — with rich text and embedded video. Enroll individuals or entire groups. Auto-enroll new group members. Progress shows on the dashboard.

  • Unlimited courses, modules, lessons, quizzes
  • Multiple choice or narrative questions
  • Native portal learning area
  • Stop trading time for money — your wisdom keeps working offline
💳

Billing, payments & contracts

Stripe-backed invoices and subscriptions. Line-item billing with branded email templates. E-signature contracts with sample agreements pre-loaded. Payment history per client. Predictable income, fewer awkward money conversations.

  • One-time + recurring subscription plans
  • Hosted invoice fallback for clients
  • E-sign coaching agreements built in
  • Receipts attached to client records, not your inbox
📣

Communication & broadcast

Branded broadcast email to active clients, archived clients, or prospects — filterable by group. Attach files from coach storage or Google Drive picker. Postmark-backed delivery. No separate email tool.

  • Group-filtered broadcasts
  • Email preview before sending
  • Prospect outreach log on every lead
  • Branded headers, colors, alignment
📊

Reports & analytics

Monthly stats. Coach/client productivity. Attendance + missing sessions reports. Onboarding completion. Feedback. Prospect-source & pipeline reports. Custom report builder with CSV export.

  • Operational dashboard for multi-coach companies
  • Aggregated calendar view across the team
  • Per-source ROI on prospect channels
  • The numbers you used to fake-confidence on calls — now real

AI in the session

Gemini-powered. Not a chatbot — woven into the workflow. Ask AI How To answers feature questions inside the app. Notes & homework clean-up. Auto-generated accountability questions. Session focus suggestions.

  • "This week's focus" suggestion before each call
  • One-click rewrite for clarity and tone
  • Question generation from session content
  • In-app AI assistant trained on the product
🧾

Assessments & evaluations

A real assessment system, not just session Q&A. Performance & evaluation library of templates. Custom assessments. Assign to clients. View responses in admin. Rename labels to match your terminology.

  • Ready-made evaluation templates
  • Starter assessments for new accounts
  • Custom terminology across coach + portal
  • Intake, mid-engagement, and outcome measurement
🏷️

Branding & white-label

Logo, header colors, alignment across emails and portal. Multi-coach teams under one company. Per-coach permissions for billing, reports, courses. Delegated admin. Optional public coach directory. Custom SMTP.

  • Partner-specific URLs for multi-tenant deployments
  • Rich coach profiles with tags, testimonials
  • Granular section access for delegates
  • Coach directory drives inbound leads

Integrations & automation

Google Calendar + Outlook 365 two-way sync. Outbound webhooks on session complete, client created, invoice paid, assignment added. API keys for custom integrations. Built-in endpoints for GoHighLevel and Groove.cm.

  • Stripe for payments & Connect-style coach billing
  • Google Drive picker for broadcast attachments
  • Per-client Zoom links on profiles
  • Webhook + API access included, not upsold

File storage & resources

Central document library for PDFs, Office docs, images, lesson plans. Files associate with clients. Open lesson plans directly inside live sessions. Sample contracts pre-loaded.

  • Mark files as lesson plans for fast session access
  • Share files with specific clients via portal
  • Quota-based storage included
  • Files live next to the conversation, not in Dropbox

Onboarding & extras

Multi-step onboarding packages with rich text + attachments. Convert-prospect flow with customizable welcome email. Comments on homework. Two-way comment workflows. Role-based access. In-app help center.

  • Send onboarding packages on import
  • Token-based comment threads on assignments
  • Standard auth + role-based access
  • Help center mirrors the product, in-app
