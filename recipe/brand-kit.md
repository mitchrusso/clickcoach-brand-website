# Brand Kit — ClickCoach

## Identity
- **Brand name:** ClickCoach (ClickCoach.io)
- **Founder:** Mitch Russo — sold software company for $10M+, ex-CEO of Tony Robbins / Chet Holmes coaching division (50+ coaches), 25 years coaching founders & execs.
- **Positioning:** The coaching operating system. Doesn't help you "run a coaching business" — helps you stop carrying the business in your head.
- **Tagline candidates:** "Run your entire coaching practice on one login." / "Stop carrying your coaching business in your head." / "The coaching OS."

## Pricing
- **$497/year per coach** — flat, all features unlocked, unlimited clients.
- Replaces $1,500–$3,000/yr of typical stack (Calendly + Stripe + Notion + ConvertKit + Typeform + LMS + Dropbox).
- Additional coaches in same company: $497/yr each.

## Brand colors (Azure Stream)
- **Primary:** `#2463EB` (vibrant blue) — CTAs, links, brand markers
- **Secondary:** `#0F172A` (deep navy) — nav, headings, footers
- **Tertiary / Accent:** `#D9F99D` (lime) — Sign-Up button, "New" badges
- **Surface:** `#F8FAFC` / **Surface subtle:** `#F1F5F9`
- **Text:** `#0C0C0D` main / `#475569` muted
- **Outlines:** `#E2E8F0`
- **Status:** `#10B981` success / `#E11D48` error

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat 700 — display 48/36, headline 32/24
- **Body / UI:** Plus Jakarta Sans 400/600 — body 18/16, label 14
- Tighten letter-spacing on display sizes; line-height 1.6 for body.

## Voice
- Edgy, direct-response sharp. Founder voice — plain-spoken, contrarian, evidence-led.
- Short sentences. Specific numbers over adjectives. No coaching theater. No corporate SaaS pablum.

## Creative direction (user)
> "We're trying to create a website that looks like https://emailoctopus.com/"

EmailOctopus reference: bold hero with friendly illustration, simple feature grid, transparent pricing block, customer logos, plain-English microcopy, lots of whitespace.
