# Creative Direction — User Note

> "We're trying to create a website that looks like https://emailoctopus.com/"

## How this shapes the build
- Hero: bold, friendly headline + concise sub + two CTAs (primary trial, secondary demo). Big floated product-UI illustration to the side.
- Section rhythm: alternating white / surface-subtle bands. 80px section gaps.
- Feature grid: simple 3-up cards with single-line headline + 1-sentence support. Icon in a soft blue circle.
- Pricing: single transparent card, large $497/yr, full feature list with green checks.
- Social proof: customer quote carousel + small logo strip ("trusted by coaches at…").
- Footer: dark navy (#0F172A), columnar sitemap, lime "Start free trial" repeat CTA.

EmailOctopus's exact illustration style isn't required — we substitute real coaching photography and product screenshots, in keeping with `imageDirectorRole = designer`.
