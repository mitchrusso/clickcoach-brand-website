# Industry Analysis — Alex

## Industry
B2B SaaS — **Coaching Operations Platform** (practice management for professional coaches, trainers, consultants).

## Audience
Independent and small-team professional coaches, 35–60 years old, 10+ years of prior career experience. Many are founders/execs who transitioned into coaching. Credentialed (ICF/PCC/MCC) or self-taught from real-world results. They are pattern-seekers and helpers — take detailed notes, believe in homework and accountability, allergic to coaching theater. They've been burned by bloated software that overpromised.

### Pain stack
1. **Operational drag** — too many tools, too many tabs, 20+ min/session in admin.
2. **Proof deficit** — they get results but can't *show* them visually.
3. **Pipeline anxiety** — without proof, sales calls stall, pricing stays low.
4. **Cognitive load** — carrying every client's history in their head.

## Tone
Edgy, direct-response sharp — never corporate. Founder voice (Mitch Russo): plain-spoken, evidence-led, slightly contrarian. EmailOctopus-style clarity — short sentences, specific numbers, generous whitespace.

## Top conversion goals
1. Start a free trial / sign up at **$497/yr** (primary CTA on every page).
2. Watch the product demo / take the tour.
3. Book a discovery call with Mitch (high-ticket coaching adjacent).
4. Browse / join the public coach directory.
5. Apply to the affiliate program.

## Recommended page count
**7 pages** — Home, Features, Pricing, For Coaches (who it's for), About (founder story), Testimonials, Contact.

## Niche match
No bespoke niche design.md selected — using user-uploaded `DESIGN-ClikcCoach.md` ("Azure Stream") as the law.

## Creative direction note
User explicitly referenced **emailoctopus.com** as visual benchmark. That site is the gold standard for "friendly utility SaaS" — bold hero, simple feature blocks, transparent pricing, customer logos, plain microcopy, abundant whitespace. We adopt that layout philosophy and overlay the Azure Stream palette.
