The hidden leak

Most coaching work never makes it into the record.

Client prep, quick research, proposal follow-up, reviewing homework, internal admin, and support messages all take time. SlipMeter gives that work a simple place to land, without forcing you back into a full dashboard every time you do five minutes of client work.

How it works

A tiny timer with useful coaching context.

SlipMeter is built to stay light, fast, and out of the way.

1

Choose a client

Select from your ClickCoach clients or add local clients if you use SlipMeter independently.

2

Pick the activity

Track prep, follow-up, admin, client session work, program design, or your own activity list.

3

Save the slip

Mark work as billable, non-billable, internal/admin, or included at no charge.

4

Report on it

Review detailed slips, summaries by client, and the time patterns that shape your week.

For ClickCoach subscribers

SlipMeter can connect to your coaching database.

When a subscriber signs in with ClickCoach, SlipMeter can use their client list, activities, and coaching context so the timer feels native instead of separate.

  • Uses your existing ClickCoach username and password
  • Reads ClickCoach clients when access is enabled
  • Saves slips in a way that can support future reporting and billing workflows
  • Included free for active ClickCoach subscribers during beta
