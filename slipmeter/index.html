The hidden leak

Most coaching work never makes it into the record.

Client prep, quick research, proposal follow-up, reviewing homework, internal admin, and support messages all take time. SlipMeter gives that work a simple place to land, without forcing you back into a full dashboard every time you do five minutes of client work.

Frequently asked questions

Top questions from coaches using SlipMeter without ClickCoach.

Can I use SlipMeter if I am not a ClickCoach subscriber?

Yes. SlipMeter can run as a standalone Mac app. You can add your own clients, activities, rounding preferences, and local time records without needing a ClickCoach subscription.

What kind of coaching work should I track with SlipMeter?

SlipMeter is designed for the work that happens around coaching sessions, including prep, follow-up, client support, homework review, admin, research, program design, and billable or non-billable client work.

How much does SlipMeter cost for standalone users?

Standalone users can start with a 30-day trial. After the trial, SlipMeter is planned at $29.99 per year.

Does SlipMeter replace my coaching platform or CRM?

No. SlipMeter is a lightweight timer and reporting tool for coach-client work. It is meant to help you capture time clearly, not replace your scheduling, CRM, billing, or full coaching platform.

Can I separate billable, non-billable, and included time?

Yes. SlipMeter is built around slips that can be marked as billable, non-billable, internal/admin, or included at no charge so you can see where your time is actually going.

If I have multiple team members using SlipMeter, can we consolidate that data?

Absolutely. Every SlipMeter app has full export capabilities, so you can combine team data in a spreadsheet or database, create reports, or upload it directly into ClickCoach. ClickCoach also includes a full SlipMeter reporting dashboard, giving your coaching business one place to review and work with the combined data.
