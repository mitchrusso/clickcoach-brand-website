The outcome arc

What happens in week 1, in 90 days, in 12 months.

Outcomes show up in a predictable sequence. We've watched it run, over and over, for years.

Week 1
Immediate relief from session admin. Notes get written faster. Homework no longer needs chasing. The client experience visibly upgrades because every client now has a portal, a dashboard, and a clear picture of what was agreed.
30–60 days
Measurable progress data begins to accumulate. The coach can hand a client a chart showing movement across six dimensions — not their opinion of progress, the data.
90 days
Those charts and session histories convert naturally into testimonials and case studies. The coach starts using them in sales conversations. Closing rates rise. Pricing rises. Inbound prospects get better, because the proof speaks before the coach does.
6–12 months
The practice begins to compound. Higher-paying clients refer more higher-paying clients. Fewer hours of work, more revenue. The business is no longer dependent on the coach's memory or willpower — it runs on a system.
